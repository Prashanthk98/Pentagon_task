2). Average Score
There are N players with their score in last 3 matches. You will have to create a dictionary and output the average score of the player asked upto 2 decimal places Print the average score of the player asked.

	Input Format

	The first line of input consist of number of players, N
	Next N lines consists of player name and their score in last three matches space separately.
	The last line of the input consist of the player in question.

Sample TestCase

	input

	3
	Warner 150 100 120
	Kohli 10 30 20
	Rohit 60 80 40
	Kohli

	output

	20.00
